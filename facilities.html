Facilities

Classrooms

The DCST department has well-equipped classrooms to cater to the needs of students and faculty.

  • Smart Classroom: The department has a smart classroom with interactive whiteboards, projectors, and other multimedia tools.

Labs

The DCST department has well-equipped labs and classrooms to cater to the needs of students and faculty. These include:

  • Computer Labs: There are multiple computer labs with state-of-the-art equipment and software, including programming tools, design software, and simulation tools.
  • Networking Labs: The department has a dedicated networking lab for students to learn about networking protocols and practices.
    • Learn More

Computers

The department has a large number of computers available for students and faculty. These include:

  • Desktop Computers: The department has multiple desktop computers available for students and faculty to use for programming, research, and other tasks.
  • Laptops: Laptops are available for students and faculty to borrow for use within the department.
  • Server Room: The department has a dedicated server room with high-performance servers for various purposes, including hosting web applications and databases.
  • High-Performance Computing Cluster: The department has a high-performance computing cluster with several nodes for parallel processing and scientific computing.
    • Learn More

With these facilities, the DCST department provides an excellent learning and research environment for students and faculty alike.
